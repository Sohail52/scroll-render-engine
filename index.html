ENGINEERING THE SCROLL ENGINE

  • Preloading Strategy: 242 frame images eagerly loaded into memory to prevent layout shifts or flashing during intense scroll behavior.
  • ScrollTrigger Progress Mapping: Mathematical binding of GSAP progress (0.0 to 1.0) normalized cleanly to the 0-241 integer frame sequence.
  • requestAnimationFrame Loop: Offloads heavy canvas bitmap draws directly into the browser's native repaint cycle for unblocked 60fps performance.
  • Dual Canvas Architecture: Foreground renders pixel-perfect bounded 'contain' scaling, while a background canvas is blurred and scaled up 110% to completely eliminate any viewport black bars.
  • DevicePixelRatio Scaling: Calculating physical pixels versus CSS representation to ensure zero blurry artifacts on Retina and 4K screens.
  • Debounced Event Listeners: Window resizes are forcefully throttled by 250ms timeouts to avoid destructive cascading render thrashing.
